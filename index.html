Frequently Asked Questions

What is iTextutors and how does it work?

iTextutors is a platform that connects tutors with students for both home and online tuition. Tutors receive student details upon selection and a reasonable service charge.

What are the charges for getting student details?

Tutors can access student details for a small fee ranging from ₹597 to ₹999, depending on the student’s grade and requirements.

How do I register as a tutor?

You can sign up on our website, select a student, pay a small fee, and start tutoring immediately.

What subjects can I teach?

Tutors can teach a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, Science, English, Social Studies, and more for different grade levels.

Is iTextutors available in my city?

We operate in major metropolitan areas including Pune, Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi NCR, Gurgaon, Kolkata, and nearby locations.

How do students pay tutors?

Students pay tutors directly based on agreed-upon fees. iTextutors does not manage student-to-tutor payments.

What happens if I don’t find a student after payment?

Our system ensures tutors receive suitable student contacts. However, if a match is not found within a reasonable time, support is available.
